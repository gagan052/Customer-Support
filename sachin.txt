Rail Mantralaya (Congress Paksh)

1.⁠ ⁠Swachhta aur Hygiene
	•	BJP ne “Swachh Rail, Swachh Bharat” ka naara diya, lekin vastavikta yeh hai ki kai sthanon par railway station gande pade hain.
	•	Tathya: CAG report 2023 ke anusar, railway stations par safai ke liye diye gaye budget ka 30% tak upyog hi nahi hua.
	•	Bogiyon me gandagi, paani ki kami aur bio-toilet aksar kharab rahte hain.
	•	Congress ka drishtikon: Hum ek “Rail Swachhta Nigrani Samiti” banayenge, jo har train aur station ki mahinaik safai report online dalegi.

⸻

2.⁠ ⁠Bheed-bhaad aur Suraksha
	•	BJP ne Bullet Train ke sapne dikhaye, par aam janta aaj bhi dabkar train me safar kar rahi hai.
	•	Tathya: NCRB data 2022 me dikhaya gaya ki railway tracks par 13,000 se adhik logon ki maut hui.
	•	Balasore (Odisha) rail haadsa 2023 me 288 logon ki jaan gayi—yeh spasht roop se suraksha me laaparwahi ka parinaam tha.
	•	Congress ka drishtikon: Hum sabse pehle suraksha, signal system aur aam yatri trains ke vistaar par dhyaan denge, na ki kewal Bullet Train par.

⸻

3.⁠ ⁠Aarakshan aur Reservation Samasyaen
	•	BJP ke dauron me reservation aur waiting list ka samasya sabse badi hai.
	•	Tathya: IRCTC data 2024 ke hisaab se 30% se zyada long-distance trains me hamesha waiting ticket rehte hain.
	•	Mahilaon, vriddhon aur chhatron ko milne wale quota me bhi aspaashtata hai.
	•	Congress ka drishtikon: Hum reservation system ko digital roop se pardarshi banayenge, aur nayi trains badhakar waiting tickets khatam karenge.

⸻

4.⁠ ⁠Aadharbhut Dhancha aur Aadhunikikaran
	•	BJP ne kaha ki railway 100% electrified ho jayega, lekin aaj bhi kai routes par diesel engines chal rahe hain.
	•	Vande Bharat aur Bullet Train dikhawa ban gayi hai—ticket mehange, trains simit routes par.
	•	Poorv aur Uttar-Poorv Bharat abhi bhi pichda hai.
	•	Congress ka drishtikon:
	•	Sabse pehle aam janta ko sasti aur surakshit yatra mile.
	•	Gramin aur pichhade kshetron tak nai trains ka vistaar ho.
	•	“Digital Railway”—WiFi, CCTV aur online shikayat pranaali ko sudhara jaye.

⸻

Bhashan ka Andaz
	•	“BJP ne ghoshnaayein ki, lekin vastavikta me railway janta ki samasyaon se joojh raha hai.”
	•	“Bullet Train aur Vande Bharat ke pradarshan se aam nagrik ki yatra surakshit nahi ho gayi.”
	•	“Railway desh ki jivan rekha hai, aur Congress isse pradarshan ka madhyam nahi, balki janta ki seva ka saadhan banayegi.”
